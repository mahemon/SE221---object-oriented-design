In “Mina Bazaar Super Store” customers buy products and salespeople sell products. 
For this purpose, some information like product’s id, name, and price and product 
availability (true/false), customer’s name, id, address and salesperson’s name and id are stored.
All customers need to pay 2% tax on the total value of their purchase.
There are two types of customers-regular customer and member customer.
Member customers get 5% discount on the total purchased value. 
When a customer buys a product, the product availability is checked before that. 


•	Design class diagram from the above story. 
•	Design appropriate classes considering the above scenario by implementing encapsulation and maximum code reusability.	
•	Find out the common activities of both types of customers. Using object oriented concept, make a way so that those activities are implemented according to the behaviors of the specific type of customers – regular and member.
•	From Main class, create multiple objects of the classes and demonstrate their behaviors.
